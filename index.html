about

I am a high school student with a passion for learning about the world around me. Through school clubs, I combine my academic interests with my eagerness to help my community and change it for the better. With the rising emphasis on computer science and the gender imbalance in tech sectors, I co-founded a Girls Who Code club on campus. GWC has allowed me to build a space where girls bolster each other, work collaboratively to solve problems in all aspects of life, and provide a strong support network.


I love baking, cooking, jotting down notes in book margins - anything that involves creating something with my hands.


Currently, I am taking college courses at my local community college to fuel my curiosity. While I am still exploring, I hope to study engineering in the future. Just the thought of creating something that could make someone else's life better excites me.
