the tl;dr

Currently, I'm exploring Physics and Computer Science at Berkeley, though I'm always adding new disciplines to my "To Investigate" list. While I'm still looking for my "niche," I'm eager to take on new challenges as learning opportunities!

My goal is to combine tech, science, and education for social change. Through my work with leading a Girls Who Code club chapter, mentoring students in science classes at Summit Public Schools: K2, running a CS education program for elementary school girls, I believe in building strong connections through mentorship, increasing STEM accessibility, and making a positive impact on the people I meet and work with.
